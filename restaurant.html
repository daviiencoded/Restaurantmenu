Delicious Bites Menu

Our menu is a refined celebration of contemporary cuisine, crafted with the finest seasonal ingredients and presented with artistic flair. Each dish is thoughtfully designed to balance bold flavors, sophisticated textures, and elegant plating — offering a dining experience that is both indulgent and unforgettable. From delicate starters to decadent desserts, every item reflects our commitment to culinary excellence and modern gastronomic innovation.

Appetizers

Our appetizers are thoughtfully crafted to awaken the palate with bold flavors and elegant presentation. From the rich, velvety lobster bisque to the delicate balance of textures in the ahi tuna tartare, each starter offers a sophisticated introduction to the culinary experience ahead. Whether it's the earthy indulgence of truffle burrata or the comforting crispness of duck confit croquettes, our opening dishes set the tone for a memorable and luxurious dining journey.Here are some of our dishes:

  • Truffle Burrata with Heirloom Tomatoes

    • Creamy burrata drizzled with white truffle oil, paired with vibrant heirloom tomatoes and micro basil, finished with a balsamic reduction.

  • Lobster Bisque with Cognac Cream

    • A rich and velvety lobster bisque, delicately infused with cognac and finished with a swirl of spiced cream and fresh chives.

  • Duck Confit Croquettes

    • Crispy croquettes filled with tender duck confit, served on a bed of arugula with cherry compote and grain mustard aioli.

  • Ahi Tuna Tartare with Avocado Mousse

    • Finely diced sashimi-grade tuna layered with avocado mousse and sesame crisps, topped with yuzu-soy vinaigrette and tobiko.

Main Courses

Our main courses are a showcase of culinary artistry, combining premium ingredients with refined techniques to create dishes that are both comforting and elevated. From the perfectly pan-seared filet mignon with its rich red wine jus to the delicate sea bass finished with a saffron beurre blanc, each entrée is thoughtfully composed for balance and depth. Vegetarian offerings like the wild mushroom risotto highlight seasonal produce and bold, earthy flavors, while the herb-crusted lamb rack delivers a classic with a modern, elegant twist. Here are some of our foods:

  • Pan-Seared Filet Mignon with Red Wine Jus

    • A tender cut of filet mignon served with a velvety red wine reduction, garlic potato purée, and roasted baby vegetables.

  • Seared Sea Bass with Saffron Beurre Blanc

    • Crispy-skinned sea bass atop a bed of leek risotto, finished with a delicate saffron beurre blanc and herb oil.

  • Wild Mushroom Risotto with Parmesan Crisp

    • Arborio rice slow-cooked with porcini, chanterelle, and shiitake mushrooms, finished with truffle oil and topped with a parmesan tuile.

  • Herb-Crusted Lamb Rack with Minted Pea Purée

    • Frenched lamb rack crusted in fresh herbs and Dijon, served with minted pea purée, honey-glazed carrots, and a rosemary demi-glace.

Desserts

Our desserts are the perfect finale to an indulgent meal, offering a blend of rich flavors, delicate textures, and artistic flair. Each sweet creation is designed to delight — from the decadent dark chocolate fondant with its molten center to the airy elegance of the passionfruit pavlova. Light yet luxurious options like the lemon lavender panna cotta provide a refreshing finish, while the salted caramel mille-feuille layers crisp pastry and silky mousse for a truly memorable treat.Here is what we offer:

  • Dark Chocolate Fondant with Vanilla Bean Ice Cream

    • A warm molten chocolate cake with a gooey center, paired with silky vanilla bean ice cream and raspberry coulis.

  • Lemon Lavender Panna Cotta

    • Silky lemon panna cotta infused with a hint of lavender, topped with a blueberry compote and almond crumble.

  • Salted Caramel Mille-Feuille

    • Delicate layers of puff pastry with salted caramel mousse and burnt sugar shards, served with crème anglaise.

  • Passionfruit Pavlova

    • Crisp meringue shell with a marshmallowy center, filled with passionfruit curd, fresh berries, and whipped Chantilly cream.
